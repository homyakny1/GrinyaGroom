BATH AND BRUSH

GroomLand's Bath and Brush service is a perfect blend of simplicity and thoroughness, offering pets a refreshing clean that keeps them looking and feeling great between more extensive grooming sessions. This service is ideal for all pets, especially those with shorter coats or less demanding grooming needs. It includes a deep cleaning bath with specially chosen shampoos and conditioners to nourish and protect your pet's coat and skin, followed by a meticulous brush-out that detangles and removes loose hair, promoting a healthier, shinier coat. Additionally, we include basic maintenance like ear cleaning and nail trimming to ensure your pet's hygiene is comprehensive.

Our approach at GroomLand is focused on making grooming a positive experience for your pet. The Bath and Brush service is designed to be quick yet thorough, minimizing stress for pets while still delivering exceptional results. By maintaining a regular grooming schedule with our Bath and Brush service, you're helping to prevent common issues such as matting, skin irritation, and excessive shedding. GroomLand is committed to providing a comfortable, caring environment where every pet can enjoy the benefits of professional grooming, ensuring they leave our facility looking well-cared-for and feeling their absolute best.
