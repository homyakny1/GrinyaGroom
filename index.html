About GroomLand

Hello and welcome to my grooming world, or as I like to call it – GroomLand, where the magic of pet grooming meets a sprinkle of sass and a whole lot of love. I'm Helen Khom, the mastermind and gentle hand behind GroomLand. With over five years of wrangling fur and turning pet grooming into a form of high art, I've been loving every moment of this adventure.

Nestled right in the lively heart of Deltona, FL, GroomLand is not your average grooming gig. It's a place where pets come to strut their stuff, get pampered, and leave with a little more swagger than they walked in with. Here, we believe that every pet deserves their moment in the spotlight, and I'm just the person to give it to them.

Armed with shears, a sense of humor, and a genuine love for all creatures furry, I've navigated the world of pet grooming with grace and a tiny bit of mischief. From competing in grooming contests that test my creativity to teaching others the art of the perfect trim in the "Skimming Me Softly" course at Groom Haüs, I've done it all. And let's be honest, if there was a PhD in making pets look fabulous, I'd probably have it by now.

At GroomLand, we're all about creating an environment that's as enjoyable for pets as it is for their humans. Whether it's a simple tidy-up or a full-on glamour makeover, my mission is to ensure your beloved companion leaves feeling like the next top model. Or at least, happier and a few pounds lighter in fur.

So, thank you for choosing GroomLand. I can't wait to meet you and your furry family member. Together, we'll make sure they leave not just looking good, but feeling like they've just had the best day at the spa. Because let's face it, they're worth it, and you probably need a break from all that pet hair at home.
