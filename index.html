About Me

Hello dear pet parents! My name is Helen Khom, and I am the heart and hands behind the esteemed grooming service here in Deltona, Florida. With over four years of professional grooming experience, I've dedicated my craft to providing gentle, precise, and tailored grooming services right in the comfort of my home-based salon.

My grooming journey began with a profound love for animals, which gradually morphed into a rewarding career. Being a certified pet groomer, I have had the privilege to partake in numerous national dog grooming competitions, clinching the top spot at the notable SuperZoo 2022 event representing GroomLand. These competitive platforms have not only honed my skills but have also fueled my passion for delivering top-notch grooming services.

One of my proudest contributions to the grooming community is my signature series shear, "Groomland By Helen." This specially designed shear, available at Groomer Supply House, embodies my commitment to excellence and precision in grooming. It's crafted to ensure every snip and trim provides a comfortable and aesthetically pleasing experience for your furry companions.

My grooming philosophy is simple yet profound: it's not just about a haircut, but creating a nurturing environment where your pets feel at ease. Every stroke of the shear, every gentle hold is a step towards making your pet look and feel their best.

The positive reinforcement from clients and the shared enthusiasm among fellow groomers have continually inspired me to elevate my grooming standards. Each day presents a new opportunity to make a difference in the lives of pets and their owners, and I am wholeheartedly committed to that cause.

I warmly welcome you and your furry family members to experience the meticulous care and expertise at my grooming haven. Let's work together to make your pets look fabulous and feel pampered. Your satisfaction and your pet's comfort are my ultimate priorities. I look forward to sharing my grooming passion and artistry with you and your beloved pets.
