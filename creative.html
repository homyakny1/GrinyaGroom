DOG CREATIVE GROOMING

At GroomLand, our Available Add-Ons service offers a curated selection of enhancements to complement and elevate the standard grooming experience. Tailored to cater to the specific needs and preferences of your pet, our range of add-ons includes ear cleaning, nail trimming, teeth brushing, paw and pad care, de-shedding treatments, flea and tick prevention, and relaxing aromatherapy sessions. Each of these services is designed with your pet's health, comfort, and well-being in mind, ensuring that they leave not just looking their best, but feeling it too. Whether it's maintaining the health of their ears, ensuring their nails are at a comfortable length, or giving their coat that extra attention to reduce shedding, our add-ons are here to provide that additional layer of care.

Our team at GroomLand understands that every pet is unique, and their grooming needs can vary widely. That's why we offer these customizable options to allow pet owners the flexibility to tailor their grooming experience according to their pet's specific requirements. The de-shedding treatment, for instance, is perfect for pets prone to heavy shedding, significantly reducing the amount of fur left around the house, while our paw and pad care is ideal for pets that frequently walk on hard or rough surfaces, providing them with the protection and relief they need. Flea and tick prevention is critical for pets' health, offering peace of mind to pet owners, whereas our aromatherapy sessions offer a soothing experience, promoting relaxation and stress relief. By selecting from our Available Add-Ons, pet owners can ensure their furry friends receive a comprehensive grooming session that addresses all aspects of their care and hygiene.
